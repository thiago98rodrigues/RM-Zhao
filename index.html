Sometime during the first half of 2005 Ocrat.com, a helpful site for learning the Chinese language, went down and was replaced with the message "OCRAT.COM is under construction. This site does not exist at this time". Because I thought that the site was so useful and it is a shame to see such a good site go offline, I want to bring back the content of the Ocrat site. In late 2005 I downloaded the content from Archive.org and started re-hosting the resources that were available.
